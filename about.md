Hi, I'm **M. Rafi Akbar**, also known as **0xrwise**.

This is my personal space on the internet where I document thoughts, research, and reflections on topics that interest me—primarily around **technology and law**. Outside of writing, I spend much of my time studying, experimenting, and learning more about the evolving relationship between **technology, digital systems, and legal structures**.
